How to set up your Wunderbuild email signature (Gmail)

What this page does: You type your details once, this page builds your Wunderbuild signature for you, and you paste it into Gmail.

Important: Do this on a computer (laptop/desktop), not on your phone. Gmail on desktop is where the full signature gets set properly.

Chrome note: Gmail in Chrome sometimes forces link underlines when you paste signatures. We harden the HTML, but Gmail can still override styling during paste. If you see underlines in the Gmail editor, send yourself a test email and check the received email too.

Part A — Build your signature on this page

  1. Fill in the required fields: Full name and Email address.

    If you leave either blank, the Copy for Gmail button stays disabled.

  2. Optional fields: Fill in anything else you want included (job title, mobile, CTAs, social links).

    • If you clear a field completely, that item will disappear from your signature (no empty lines).
    • Some fields are already filled with Wunderbuild defaults (landline, website, office address, social links). Only change these if you’ve been told to.

  3. Check the Live Preview on the right. That’s what gets copied into Gmail.

  4. Click: ✅ Copy for Gmail

    You should see a “Copied” message. If copy fails, follow the Manual copy steps shown under the button.

Part B — Paste your signature into Gmail (computer)

  1. Open Gmail in your browser.

  2. Open Settings:

    • Top right → click the gear icon (Settings)
    • Click See all settings

  3. Find the Signature section:

    • Stay on the General tab
    • Scroll down to Signature

  4. Paste: click inside the signature box → paste with Ctrl+V (Windows) or Cmd+V (Mac).

    Do not use “paste without formatting”.

  5. Save: scroll to the bottom → click Save Changes.

Please don’t do these things 🙏

  • Don’t type the signature manually.
  • Don’t delete random icons/buttons in Gmail. If you want something removed, clear the field here and copy again.
  • Don’t try to “fix” fonts or spacing inside Gmail after pasting.
